Who We Are

TutorMe was created in mind to help students struggling in a course with a qualified tutor that knows the relevant subject and has at least taken the course or course equivalent in the past. The TutorMe team knows that it is sometimes hard to find highly qualified tutors at affordable prices so we take care of that part for you. You can sign up to be a student or tutor here and get connecting right away. Happy learning!

Please note: Tutor availability is limited. For the best chance of finding a suitable tutor, sign up early in the semester. Most tutors are not available during final exam periods or over the spring/summer intersession. Please plan accordingly.
