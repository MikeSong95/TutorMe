What is TutorMe?
We are a tutor matching service catering to university students in Canada. If you need academic help in a subject you can find private tutoring with a qualified tutor in TutorMe system.
How do I sign up as a tutor?
You can click here to sign up and select Tutor on the first screen
How do I sign up as a student?
You can click here to sign up and select Student on the first screen
What courses and programs do you offer tutoring services for?
Tutoring is available in most major programs for most courses. Sign up here and check if there are any current tutors available for the courses that you need help in.
How much do I get paid?
We allow tutors to set their own prices. The current average fee for tutoring is $20/hr.
Some tutors, with extensive tutoring experience, do charge $25 and up. Please confirm, and agree to pricing in writing with your student before your first meeting.
How do I pay?
You pay the fee directly to the tutor during each session. Cash only (unless your tutor agrees to other payment methods).
Who are the tutors?
Our tutors include undergraduate students, graduate students, graduates and retired faculty. All tutors are required to have excellent grades or an extensive background in the courses they wish to tutor.
If you’re interested in becoming a tutor, click here.
What if there is no tutor available?
While we try our best to pair you with a qualified tutor, sometimes there are simply no tutors to help in your subject or none with available time. Don’t forget that your professors should be your number one resource. Visit them for additional help during office hours, or arrange for a private meeting. Let them know that you’ve tried to find a tutor but haven’t been successful; they might be able to suggest additional options. Also contact the departmental administrator; many departments maintain tutor lists that include tutors who are not registered with the TutorMe
